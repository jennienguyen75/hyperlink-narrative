SEP 19 2017

Breed: American Shorthair (Tabby)

Birthplace: Unknown (found on the street)

Parents: Unknown

Found: September 19, 2017

He was found near Plano, Texas on September 19, 2017, at approximately three months old and was taken to a nearby shelter. Since his exact birthdate was unknown, this date was later marked as his official birthday. At the time, he had not yet been given a name.

A few days later, the shelter staff named him Leo and took him to the vet for a full check-up. He was found to be healthy, with no signs of illness or abnormalities. The shelter continued to care for him for several more months before he was placed in a new foster home.
