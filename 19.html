This year, Pecan suddenly got very sick. He caught a cold that wouldn’t go away, leaving him weak and tired. One day, he tried to go outside, but a few crows chased and bullied him. At the time, the Nguyens didn’t notice any injuries.

A few weeks later, they realized something was wrong and decided to take him to the vet for a check-up. That’s when they learned just how important insurance would be, as Pecan needed proper care and monitoring for his FIV.
