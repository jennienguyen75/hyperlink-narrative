MAR 16 2021

After two long years without a home, Pecan finally got lucky as he was adopted by the Nguyens! His forever home had arrived, and they immediately fell in love with him. He was gentle, friendly, and one of the most polite cats they had ever met.

As he got comfortable, Pecan became more confident. One day, he decided to explore outside, but he always came back quickly, never keeping his owners waiting. He finally felt free without feeling trapped inside.

The shelter had told the Nguyens he was in perfect health and didn’t need medical supplies or insurance. They didn’t disclose his full health history. Little did the family know, Pecan’s FIV meant he might need extra care, and animal health can be just as expensive as human health.
